What it is

A unique five-day, natured-inspired, outdoor learning experience on striving to achieve and maintain serenity following a personal loss
On the-trail teaching by both a Master Life Coach and national-award winning geology professor

Goal

Finding, growing, and sharing a happy, contented life style

You may benefit if you

…have lost a loving relative or companion
…are struggling to rediscover happiness to share with others
…are curious about the origins of alpine beauty
…would like to experience nature’s healing power
…enjoy learning
