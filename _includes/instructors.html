Dr. Lee Suttner, PhD

Dr. Suttner is Emeritus Robert Shrock Professor of Earth and Atmospheric Sciences at Indiana University, Bloomington. He is a recipient of the nation’s highest teaching award bestowed by the National Association of Geoscience Teachers, the President’s Award for Outstanding Teaching at Indiana University, and the Outstanding Educator Award from the Eastern Section of the American Association of Petroleum Geologists, all in part in recognition of his excellent teaching in the field.

Dr. Suttner also has co-authored multiple award-winning, peer-reviewed journal articles related to his research on the origin and growth of major mountain ranges in the western interior of the United States.
